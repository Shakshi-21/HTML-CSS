Tribute to Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug, a pioneer in the development of high-yielding crops that helped combat hunger.

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist who is considered one of the greatest minds in the fight against hunger. His work in developing high-yielding, disease-resistant crops in the 20th century helped save millions of people from starvation and earned him the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970.

He is often referred to as "The Father of the Green Revolution" for his groundbreaking contributions to agricultural science and his efforts to eradicate hunger worldwide.
